
Youth wrestling program holding fund-raiser
 
Date Published to Web: 10/21/2004
The Alderwood Little Grappler youth wrestling program will be holding a barbecue this Saturday and Sunday.   The fund-raiser will be held at Food Pavilion, which is sponsoring the event, from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. on both days.   Proceeds from the event will go to purchase uniforms for the program.   There will be hamburgers, hot dogs and pops available as part of the barbecue.
