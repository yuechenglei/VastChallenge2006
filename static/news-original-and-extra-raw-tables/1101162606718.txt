
Five local students earn EWU degrees
 
Date Published to Web: 10/19/2004
CHENEY - Five Lower Alderwood Valley students earned degrees from Eastern Washington University at the end of summer quarter 2004.   The EWU graduates include Jeffery Monahan of Alderwood, Master of Science degree in physical education; Leticia Chavez of Outlook, Master of Social Work; Lucy Rivera, Bachelor of Arts in social work; and Prosser's Corey Phillips, Bachelor of Arts in therapeutic recreation; and Stacey Thompson, Bachelor of Arts in therapeutic recreation.
