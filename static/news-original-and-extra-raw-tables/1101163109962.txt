
Grid Kid program to have sign-ups Wednesday
 
Date Published to Web: 7/13/2003
The Alderwood Grid Kid football program will be holding sign-ups Wednesday from 6 to 7 p.m. in front of the Alderwood High School football field.   The program is open to youngsters ages 9 to 12. Interested participants will need to bring a $60 registration fee and a copy of their birth certificate.   The Grid Kid program is expected to hold a pre-season camp later this month with members of the Grizzly football team.
