


Citizen compliments Cinco de Mayo event


 


Date Published to Web: 5/14/2003


Alderwood resident Doris Brandvold complimented the Alderwood Chamber of Commerce and the city at this past Monday night's Alderwood City Council meeting.
   Brandvold said the jail trustees and chamber officials did a nice job of maintaining the downtown area where the Cinco de Mayo event was held this past weekend.
   Mayor Rex Luthor also extended his congratulations to the Alderwood chamber for what he viewed as a successful Cinco de Mayo event.
